01 — Morning

Connect your calendars once. Then forget the app.

Link Google Calendar, Google Tasks, Apple Calendar, Notion, or Taskade in the Kirole iPhone app. Your companion wakes up on the E-ink device with the day already in hand — no feed, no red badges.

Syncs quietly over Bluetooth about once an hour. E-ink means weeks of battery, not hours.

02 — Midday

Tap a task on the device. Your phone goes quiet.

One tap on the E-ink screen starts a focus session on your iPhone — no unlocking, no scrolling past three apps to get there. Your companion stays on the desk with you while you work.

Every 30 minutes of staying with it fills an energy bottle. Bottles unlock new scenes for your companion.

03 — Evening

Close the day, gently.

A soft recap — what got done, what moves to tomorrow. No guilt, no streaks to lose. The panel dims to night, and tomorrow is already loaded.

If the device was offline, nothing is lost — it remembers what you did and catches up with the app when it reconnects.

Quiet by design

Built to be trusted with your day.

  • No ads. No cross-site tracking. No analytics SDKs.
  • Your photo never leaves your device.
  • The hardware stays silent — no pings, no feeds.
  • Offline-first: the device remembers, then catches up.

How Kirole uses your Google data: if you connect Google Calendar or Google Tasks, Kirole reads your events and tasks to show your day inside the app and on your paired E-ink device, and to let your companion talk about your day.
